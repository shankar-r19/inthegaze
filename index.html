Frequently asked questions

How do I start a focus session?

Open the app and tap the focus button on the home screen. Choose your duration and begin.

What happens if I leave the app during a session?

GAZE detects when you pick up your phone or switch away and prompts you to return so your session counts.

Does the app keep my screen on?

Yes — during an active session the screen stays awake so your timer and progress stay visible.

Do I need an account?

No. Your progress is saved on your device as a guest by default.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. See our for details.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

Email us at support.inthegaze@gmail.com — we read every message.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

GAZE ("we", "us") operates the GAZE mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information we collect

GAZE is designed to work without collecting personal data. Your focus sessions, streaks and progress are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third party.

We do not collect, store or sell:

  • Names, email addresses or contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics that identify you personally

Device permissions

The App may request permission to:

  • Keep the screen awake during focus sessions
  • Send notifications to alert you when a session ends or warn you about distractions

These permissions are used solely to deliver the App's core features.

Third-party services

The App does not integrate third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
